Our Mission

With Kids@hbc, our mission is to love and lead the children of hbc into God's kingdom, partnering with their parents to help each one grow to be the adult God designed them to be.

We believe that how we receive the little children is how we receive Christ. For that reason, kids are centered in our community.

Worship and Music: Kids connect with God through joyful praise and worship.

Building Friendships: We help kids form strong, supportive friendships.

God's Word in Their Hearts: Kids learn and remember God’s truth through verses and lessons.

Learning for Every Age: We teach God’s Word in fun, age-appropriate ways.

Sunday Mornings

Join us on Sunday mornings, where kids can learn about Jesus and the Bible in a safe, age appropriate, and interactive environment. Our dedicated teachers use engaging lessons, activities, and crafts to help kids grow in their understanding of God's love and plan for their lives.
